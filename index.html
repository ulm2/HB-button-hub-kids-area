Butterfly steps to reset

Inspired by the calming butterfly theme from the binder pages, but rewritten for clearer website use.
1

Pause and land softly

Stop your body. Put both feet on the floor, chair, or cushion. You are safe.

2

Breathe in, breathe out

In through the nose for 4. Out slowly for 4. Try 5 rounds.

3

Pick one quiet tool

Choose drawing, squeezing, rocking, a stuffed animal, or another favorite calm tool.

4

Let the feeling get smaller

You do not have to fix everything at once. Just help your body settle a little.

5

Return when ready

When your body feels safer, you can go back, ask for support, or take one more break.

Support plan

For caregivers, teachers, or a website owner who wants this to feel personal and useful.

Helpful phrases

  • “Your body is telling us something.”
  • “Let’s make it smaller, not perfect.”
  • “Do you need quiet, movement, or comfort?”
  • “I can stay with you while you calm down.”

Ideas to customize

  • Add your daughter’s name
  • Replace icons with real photos of her tools
  • Add audio buttons for read-aloud support
  • Link one zone to one preferred strategy

Website improvements included

  • More balanced colors
  • Cleaner spacing and larger tap targets
  • Interactive saving for favorite tools
  • Progress tracker and printable pages
